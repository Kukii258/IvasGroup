About us

Passionate professionals with over 25 years of experience in the industry.

Our Story

For over 25 years, IVAS has been a leader in full production solutions, specializing in lighting, audio, and video, as well as trussing, rigging, and staging for all types of events. From local gatherings to international spectacles, our team delivers unparalleled expertise.

Our comprehensive services include location scouting, 3D rendering, and detailed event planning and management. Whether it’s a large music festival, a corporate event, or a live TV broadcast, IVAS tailors its offerings to meet the unique needs of each project.

With a robust portfolio of over 2500 projects and 500 clients worldwide, IVAS continues to innovate and provide exceptional service in the evolving field of event production.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower your events with superior technical support and creative solutions, ensuring high-quality production values that enhance every audience's experience. At IVAS, we commit to transforming each event into a memorable spectacle through meticulous planning and exceptional execution.

Our Values

  • Excellence: Aiming for excellence in every project.
  • Innovation: Leading with innovation in event technology.
  • Reliability: Reliable, on-time, and within budget.
  • Collaboration: Working closely with clients to surpass expectations.
  • Safety: Prioritizing safety in every setup.
