About us

Passionate professionals with over 25 years of experience in the industry.

Our Story

We offer full event management from location scouting, complete design, 3D rendering, virtual show programming, to detailed planning, set building and production management. IVAS provides its expertise for indoor or outdoor projects of all sizes including large music festivals, touring, fashion, corporate events, sport events as well as TV live broadcast productions. Technical skills developed after years of working many different types of events enable us to adapt to any occasion and provide a stunning experience for your audience.

Working as a part of your team, we ensure that your exact audio, video and lighting requirement are matched. Following the developments in video technology, we are constantly production support. Using the latest lighting techniques, IVAS creates original and inspiring themes and solutions for every project. Professional resources ensure that your event receives the sound and visual quality it deserves, by determining the right pitch and frequency for your audience.

Our Mission

By providing support to global brands and institutions in areas of infrastructure, production and local expertise, we give our clients the comfort to focus on their content rather than technical details.

Our greatest value is our offer – world class service at developing country prices.
